Welcome to the world of Dungeons and Dragons.

Dungeons and Dragons is a tabletop pen and paper role playing game, where players take on the role of mighty adventurers who go on fantastical quests, fighting monsters, finding treasure, and saving the realm. One player, called the Dungeon Master (DM), instead controls the world and its inhabitants to provide interesting stories and frightening challenges for the players who adventure there. In particular, the DM has the responsibility of controlling the monsters that will do battle with the players.

Generating monsters that the players will fight has a number of steps and can take time to do manually, the purpose of our program is to expedite the process, and allow a DM to instantly generate a random monster for their players to fight based on a variety of parameters. In the following scenarios, you will take on the role of a dungeon master as he prepares encounters for the players in his game.
